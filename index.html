A little introduction...

Hi, I’m Manon! Welcome to my creative space, where I share my passion for macramé and teach you how to create stunning jewelry and decorative items through step-by-step tutorials. Whether you’re a complete beginner or already have some experience, you’ll find tutorials tailored to different difficulty levels to suit your skills and goals. I’ve been passionate about macramé since I discovered it as a child at a creative fair. Over the years, I’ve honed my techniques, using durable and beautiful materials like LINHASITA waxed polyester thread to bring my designs to life. Now, I’m thrilled to share my knowledge with you, so you can craft your own unique pieces. Dive in, explore, and start creating!
